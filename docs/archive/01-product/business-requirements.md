# Business Requirements

**Project:** Asset Monitoring System
**Author:** Akinkunmi Mustapha

---

## Why This Exists

Trading212 doesn't give me enough visibility into how my portfolio is really performing. I need a single place where I can understand my investments clearly — without having to do the analysis manually.

The dashboard should remove the thinking. I should be able to look at it and immediately understand what's performing, what's at risk, and where the opportunities are.

---

## What It Needs to Do

**Portfolio oversight** — Track overall performance, returns, and risk across the whole portfolio.

**Advanced metrics** — Calculate metrics Trading212 doesn't provide: beta, daily returns per asset, volatility, drawdown, moving averages.

**Asset-level analysis** — For any given asset, I should be able to understand:
- How much I've spent (cost basis, DCA)
- What the return is — is it actually good?
- What the risk is if I buy or sell now
- Whether there's an opportunity (undervalued? good entry point?)
- How valuable it is relative to my portfolio

**Correlation tracking** — Understand how assets relate to each other to manage diversification.

**Statistical comparisons** — Compare assets across the portfolio to find patterns and relationships.
